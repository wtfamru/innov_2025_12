Welcome to Mental Health Predictor

Depression is a common illness worldwide, with more than 300 million people affected. India is home to an estimated 57 million people (18% of the global estimate) affected by depression.

Depression Statistics Worldwide

Depression is a major contributor to the global burden of disease.

  • More than 264 million people are affected by depression globally.
  • Depression is the leading cause of disability worldwide.
  • Suicide is the second leading cause of death among 15-29 year-olds.
  • Women are more likely than men to experience depression.
